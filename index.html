Common questions

Do I need to install an app? +

No. FatBotSlim works through regular Telegram — nothing extra to install. Just open the bot and start typing. Works on iOS, Android, Mac and Windows.

How accurate is the AI calorie count? +

AI gives an approximate estimate based on standard portions and composition. For home-cooked meals, accuracy is around 85–90%. You can always adjust the weight directly in the chat if needed.

Is my data safe? +

All data is stored in a secure cloud database. We never sell or share your data with third parties. Your food history is linked to your Telegram ID only.

Can I write in both Russian and English? +

Yes, the bot understands both languages. You choose the interface language (EN/RU) on first launch, but you can type food names in any language — the bot will figure it out.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription? +

Cancel anytime via the /pay command in the bot. Your access continues until the end of the paid period — no money is lost.
